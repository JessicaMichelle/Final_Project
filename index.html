The Station Cafe & The Filling Station Tap Room

Jon & Stacie Rowe started out in the 300 sq ft gas station in December of 2006. We have since moved next door and have 3000 sq ft. turning the small gas station into a tap room.

We offer house made bread, pizza, dressings, soup and desserts. The Tap Room offers 25 beers on draft some local and some just our favorites.
